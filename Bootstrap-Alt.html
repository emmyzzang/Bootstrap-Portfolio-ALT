Emmy Emmy

About Me

Emmy Emmy is a YoRHa android created to battle the machine lifeforms that have invaded the planet on behalf of the surviving humans.

Emmy is an all-purpose battle android, deployed as a member of the YoRHa infantry. She is equipped with a multitude of weapons for close quarters combat and can attack from range using the Pod support system. Her eyes are obscured beneath her standard-issue military visor, which she rarely removes.

Emmy was given orders to observe one of the new Scanner models in action during YoRHa's operations against the machines.
